Burrito Recipes

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for you to follow. It is a simple and healthy way for you to stay on track of your diet! The recipe is flexible so please make it your own along the way!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Black Beans
  • Onions
  • Spinach tortilla

Steps

  1. Cook your chicken to your liking with your own seasoning
  2. Put your chicken to the side and sautee your onion
  3. Add your black beans to the onions once the onions have caramalized
  4. Now add your cooked chicken to the onions and beans
  5. After mixing thoroughly, you can now add the ingredients ontop of your burrito
